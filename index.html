The Champion's Journey to the World Cup is a fundraising campaign to support the development of our future leaders, entrepreneurs, heroes, and champions. When you give during this campaign, PureGame Field Champions (our fully trained staff) will provide young people with the highest quality sports-based mentorship and social-emotional learning programming in Southern California!
To get involved, click a seat on the virtual stadium map to pick a donation level that inspires you. However you choose to get involved, your gift will positively impact youth in Orange County as they pave their own pathways to success!
We invite you to join us on this Champions Journey to raise funds for our amazing youth.
